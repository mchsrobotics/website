Welcome to the website of the Mount Carmel HS Robotics Club. We are an FTC team based out of San Diego, California, and have competed annually in our local FTC Competition for four years. We use a combination of programming and design work to build a robot which competes against other schools and organizations in a yearly global competition. For more information about our club, click on the side links, or view news and challenge updates below.

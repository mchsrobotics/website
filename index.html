Welcome to the website of the Mount Carmel HS Robotics Club. We are an FTC team based out of San Diego, California, and have competed annually in our local FTC Competition for four years. We use a combination of programming and design work to build a robot which competes against other schools and organizations in a yearly global competition. Team Organization


At Team Uncopywrightable, we believe that, due to larger size of our team than many of our competitiors, that having every member specialize in a specific field is most efficient, so that we don't have a large amount of our members unable to work. We divide into a build team and and a programming team, with some members opting for more specialized roles such as Design. We also have a seperate organizational system for the team as a club, and have a Logistics, PR, and IT team.

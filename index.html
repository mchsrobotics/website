Welcome to the website of MCHS Robotics!

Welcome to the website of MCHS Robotics, an FTC team based out of Mount Carmel High School, in San Diego, California. Using a multitude of both building and programming skills, we construct a robot that competes against other robots made schools and organizations also participating in the competition. For more information about our club, click on the information tab above. For a list of our current roster, including our student and adult leaders with relevant contact information, click on the roster tab. For a list of upcoming events, click on the events tab. For more about FTC, and links to FTC and other robotics information, click on links. If you have any further questions, email the webmaster at "owenpage@protonmail.ch".
